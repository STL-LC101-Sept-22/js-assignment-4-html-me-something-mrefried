How to make the best Roasted Tomato Soup!

Lets start by gathering our ingredients and tools!

Equipment:
  • Stove
  • Oven

    • Tools:
  • Cutting Board
  • Knife
  • Dutch Oven
  • Serving Spoon
  • Immersion Blender

    • Ingredients:
  • 2 tbsp Olive Oil
  • 1 Yellow Onion
  • 2 Cloves Garlic
  • 5 lbs Fresh Tomatoes
  • 4 oz Fresh Basil
  • 2 tbsp Kosher Salt
  • 1 tbsp Cracked Black Pepper
  • 2 oz Milk or Heavy Cream

    • Directions: Pre heat your oven to 350*. Place your dutch oven on the stove top and heat on medium high. While that is warming up, on your cutting board slice your onion and garlic. Add your oil to the dutch oven, once shimmering add the sliced onions and garlic. Stir with your spoon and cook until lightly caramelized. While that is cooking, remove the stems of and quarter your tomatoes. Add to the pot, put in your salt and pepper and give a quick stir. Bury all the basil and uncovered place in your oven. Let cook for 30 minutes. Carefully take out of the oven, remove the basil and add your milk or cream. Blend with the immersion blender until smooth. Taste and adjust seasoning if needed.

    Serve and Enjoy!
